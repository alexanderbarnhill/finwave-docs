## What you'll learn

- Where to find population settings and what each section controls
- How to update your population's profile, description, and banner
- How the settings sidebar is organized

## Accessing settings

Navigate to **Administration > Populations** and select your population. The settings page opens with a sidebar on the left listing all available modules.

## Settings modules

### Configuration

- **Settings** — Edit your population's display name, species, abbreviation, description, and banner image. These details appear on your population's profile throughout finwave and on the [public page](/web/administration/public-pages/) if enabled.
- **Relationships** — Manage steward and beneficiary relationships between your population and other populations.

### Data

- **Individuals** — Browse, search, and manage individuals in your population.
- **Review Animals** — Review provisionally created animals awaiting confirmation.
- **Catalog** — Access the Catalog Builder for managing population graphs (requires finwave Pro).
- **Analysis** — Run population analyses including social network analysis, discovery curves, composition breakdowns, and capture history exports (requires finwave Pro).
- **ML Center** — View ML model performance metrics for your population's detection, classification, and identification models.
- **Review Tasks** — Create and manage review tasks for collaborative data validation (requires finwave Pro).
- **Data Access** — Configure data export and API access settings.

### Public Page

- **Public Page** — Enable and configure your population's public landing page. Set up the subdomain slug, hero image, call to action, and social links. See [Public Pages](/web/administration/public-pages/) for the full guide.
- **Highlights** — Create and manage highlights (stories, papers, announcements, milestones) that appear on your public page.

### Community

- **Members** — Invite new members, assign roles, and manage existing membership.
- **Supporters** — Manage external contributors and supporters.
- **Requests** — Review and respond to membership join requests.
- **News** — Publish news articles for your population community (requires finwave Pro).
