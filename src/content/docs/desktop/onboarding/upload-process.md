:::note
The upload process is under active development and not yet available in the desktop client. This page describes the planned functionality.
:::

## Planned features

The upload process will support:

- **Background processing** -- Uploads will run in the background while you continue working in other sections of the app.
- **Resumability** -- If you close the client or lose connectivity, uploads will pick up exactly where they left off.
- **Throttling** -- Configurable bandwidth limits and concurrent upload caps to avoid saturating your network.
- **Progress tracking** -- Per-encounter status with counts for pending, in-progress, completed, and failed uploads.
- **Error recovery** -- Failed encounters can be retried individually or in bulk.
