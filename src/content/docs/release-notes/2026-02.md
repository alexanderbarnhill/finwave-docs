## Public Population Pages

Populations can now have a publicly accessible landing page at their own subdomain (e.g., `srkw.finwave.io`). These pages are search-engine-indexed and designed for researchers, conservation organizations, journalists, and the general public.

### What's included

- **Hero section** with customizable image and credit
- **Stats bar** showing key metrics (individuals, encounters, images, contributors, tracking period) computed automatically from your data
- **About section** pulled from your population description, with organization cards for each managing organization
- **Featured individuals** grid — select up to 8 individuals to highlight, or let finwave auto-select the most-encountered
- **Highlights feed** — share news, papers, announcements, and milestones on your public page
- **Call to action** — customizable CTA encouraging visitors to contribute
- **Social links** — link to your website, Instagram, Twitter, Facebook, and YouTube

### How to enable

1. Navigate to **Population Settings > Public Page**
2. Toggle the page on and enter a subdomain slug
3. Submit the slug for platform admin approval
4. Once approved, configure your hero image, featured individuals, CTA, and social links

See [Public Pages](/web/administration/public-pages/) for the full guide.

### Platform administration

Platform administrators can review and approve or reject slug requests from the new **Slug Requests** card in Platform Administration.

### Privacy

Public pages expose only curated content and aggregate statistics — no GPS coordinates, contributor details, raw encounter records, or data export capabilities.
